I was born and raised in Paris, France. In 2009, I completed a Degree in Chinese Studies (Philosophy, Literature and Arts) at [INALCO](http://www.aefe.fr/reseau-scolaire-mondial/rechercher-un-etablissement/viet-nam-ho-chi-minh-ville-lycee-francais) (National Institute for Oriental Languages and Civilizations). I was then a Chinese teacher in several high schools for five years. This has been a very enriching experience, especially at [Saigon French International Highschool in Vietnam](http://www.aefe.fr/reseau-scolaire-mondial/rechercher-un-etablissement/viet-nam-ho-chi-minh-ville-lycee-francais).

The first time I heard about Bitcoin was in 2011. I started to test it and design several decentralized apps from April 2013.

I worked for an iOS payment app as a sales and community outreach, and we had the chance to discover Vitalik's Ethereum white paper in December 2013.

In 2014, I joined the marketing team of [OpenClassrooms](https://openclassrooms.com/), one of the leading e-learning companies in Europe. I set up the Customer Service from scratch, handling more than 1,000 requests a week.

I led the crowdsale campaign of [iExec](https://iex.ec/): we raised 10,000 BTC on April 19, 2017 ($12 m eq. back then). I was iExec Head of Communications on this project for about 3 years.

I'm the founder of [Strat](https://strat.cc), which mission is to amplify the Web3 movement. I taught Marketing Strategy at [EM Lyon](https://www.em-lyon.com/en), one of the most reputed business schools in France. I gave a hand at kickstarting [Interlude](http://interlude.gg/), and other gaming projects.

I'm a volunteer at [Emmaüs Connect](https://emmaus-connect.org/), an organisation focusing on digital inclusion, and I also contribute on several different projects as a Web3 hacker (Solidity, ReactJS, and TypeScript mainly), including [Concord](https://github.com/allforclimate/concord): a new form of DAO controlled from Discord. The project was developed under the good care of [All for Climate DAO](https://dao.allforclimate.earth/). We won the 1st prize at the [DAO Global Hackathon](https://hackforfreedom.org/#winners).

In 2020, I co-founded [Āto](https://ato.works/) which provides top-level IP licenses for NFTs and makes legal easy for the whole Web3 community. I've built several NFT-related apps used by artists, auction houses and fine art galleries.

As a Developer Relations Engineer, I worked for [Aurora](https://aurora.dev/) and [Arthera](https://docs.arthera.net/). As a dev, I also worked on an NFT Registry for an institutional partner.

I co-founded the [W3HC](https://w3hc.org/) (Web3 Hackers Collective), a DAO that focuses on Web3 integrations, mentoring and learning. I'm developing Gov, an on-chain voting system designed for everyday people.
